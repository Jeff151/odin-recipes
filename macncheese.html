Macoroni and Cheese--Please!!!

This recipe is my all time favorite. It made the list because it's not only fast, but delicious too!

  • Box of Great Value Macoroni and Cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt and Pepper (if desired)

Steps

  1. Open package, set aside cheese packet
  2. Cover noodles with water and boil
  3. Drain noodle and add 1/4 stick real butter, stir til melted
  4. Stir in cheese packets and 1/8 cup milk
  5. Mix well, season to taste

This is the worlds greatest mac and cheese. If you folloiw these directions exactly, you could win an award for a world-class recipe. Stock up on this, your family will want it every day!
