Pita Pizza for all!

This pita pizza recipe is perfect for the busy student because it can be made in the microwave with only 3 ingredients plus optional toppings.

  • Pitas
  • Pizza Sauce (or spaghetti sauce)
  • Mozzarella Cheese (shredded)
  • Toppings (optional)

Steps

  1. Start with a pita
  2. Add pizza sauce
  3. Sprinkle with desired amount of shredded mozzarella.
  4. Microwave until cheese is melted. Mine took about a minute. Lighter layer of cheese would need less time.

That’s it! Just 3 ingredients plus optional toppings, and it can even be made in a microwave. It just doesn’t get any easier.
